Welcome In our GYM

A modern day gymnasium
(as gym used to be called way back in Ancient Greece) is a place for indoor physical workout where various equipment and machines are typically used. For some people, a typical gym is a place where you focus on weight lifting and similar activities.



Regular exercise and physical activity promotes strong muscles and bones. It improves respiratory, cardiovascular health, and overall health. Staying active can also help you maintain a healthy weight, reduce your risk for type 2 diabetes, heart disease, and reduce your risk for some cancers.



In addition to building muscle and losing weight, gym workouts can help you improve or maintain your balance, gait and agility.
A key benefit of a gym is the variety of equipment it offers, letting you work out using different footwork, speed, agility and balance movements.

KEEP GOING

Going to the gym can be considered a sport if it is physically challenging for you, is done for entertainment (not out of obligation) and involves a competitive element against yourself or others, such as following a progressive program, tracking your weights or entering a bodybuilding competition
